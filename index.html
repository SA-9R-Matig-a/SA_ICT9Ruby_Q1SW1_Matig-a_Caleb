Attack on Titan: A Timeless Masterpiece


Attack on Titan is...

An AniManga created by Hajime Isayama that follows the story of Eren Jaeger.
We encounter: various themes of humanity and conflict, a large and rich cast of main and supporting characters, human and understandable antagonists, massive drama, and lots of engaging action.

There is NO episode in the whole series that will bore you once you're invested.


Why I love Attack on Titan so much

The characters are so easy to care about!
I found myself so invested in a lot of characters, and got scared of seeing them in situations where they may not make it to the next episode. A character I particularly liked was Scout Commander: Erwin Smith, a cunning and genius leader of the Survey Corps.
The story is very compelling
Attack on Titan's story is one of the best I have ever consumed in my life. I would put it in either the top 3 or even the top 1 on my list of "Best Narratives I Have Seen".
The soundtrack is awesome
Listening to the soundtrack is other-worldly. The music just accompanies and complements every moment so well. I listen to some songs from the OST, I'm even listening to one right now!
